I recreated the iPhone Edit Alarm screen. On the version above,
users are forced to scroll through every number until they reach the one they want.
Instead of scrolling through all numbers, I created a design that would allow users
to see every number on the screen at one time that they could click without scrolling (link below).
The screen displays each number in three separate sections: hour, tens-minute,
and ones-minute. Users can select an alarm time by clicking the desired numbers
without scrolling. When they are finished, they can select ‘Save’ to be notified
their alarm is set. The app requires five clicks in completion: hour, tens-minute,
ones-minute, AM/PM, and Save.
